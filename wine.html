Rkatsiteli

Amber Wine

Welcome to the heart of Anaberika Winery, where artistry meets tradition, and nature's bounty is transformed into liquid gold by our visionary head winemaker, Ana Berikashvili. In this verdant corner of the world, nestled amid the picturesque vineyards of Georgia, Ana pours her soul into crafting wines that sing with the essence of the land.

Among the many treasures of Anaberika's cellar, the Rkatsiteli wine stands as a true testament to Ana's unparalleled expertise and dedication. With delicate finesse, she orchestrates a symphony of flavors from the indigenous Rkatsiteli grape, a variety as ancient as the land from which it springs. Rkatsiteli, revered for its high acidity and remarkable adaptability, is like a canvas for Ana's artistic expressions. She carefully harvests these golden-hued grapes, embracing the whispers of sun-kissed terroirs and the secrets of centuries-old vines.

In the depths of our qvevris, traditional clay vessels buried beneath the Georgian soil, the alchemy unfolds. The grape clusters, whole and vibrant, find their sanctuary within the embrace of these earthen amphorae. Here, the grape skins and seeds dance with the juice, giving birth to a wine that bears the hallmark amber glow.

As time weaves its gentle magic, Ana patiently tends to each qvevri, coaxing the Rkatsiteli into revealing its truest essence. The result is a wine that captivates the senses — an opus of flavors, a tapestry of aromas. Imagine the zest of ripe citrus harmonizing with the subtle sweetness of orchard fruits, kissed by the gentle caress of wild herbs.

In every sip of our Rkatsiteli wine, one tastes not just the grape but the story of generations. It's the embodiment of tradition, the song of our soil, and Ana's masterpiece—a testament to the tireless passion that infuses every bottle crafted at Anaberika Winery. Come, uncork a piece of history, and let the artistry of Ana Berikashvili take you on a journey through the vineyards of Georgia. Cheers to a wine that embodies a timeless tale, a wine that is Rkatsiteli in its purest form.

Saperavi

Red Wine

Venture into the enigmatic realm of Anaberika Winery, where the passionate spirit of our master winemaker, Ana Berikashvili, breathes life into the velvety depths of Saperavi, a jewel in the crown of Georgian winemaking.

In the embrace of ancient Georgian vineyards, where history whispers through the grapevines, Ana meticulously tends to the Saperavi vine, a varietal as deep and rich as the dark earth that nurtures it. Guided by tradition and driven by innovation, Ana orchestrates a symphony of flavors that captivate the senses and leave an indelible mark on the soul.

Saperavi, a grape celebrated for its inky hue and robust character, becomes Ana's canvas. As the sun paints the skies with hues of twilight, she carefully plucks these mystical grapes, each one a promise of what is to come.

Into the qvevri they go, these precious berries, yielding to the call of the clay vessel. The qvevri, ancient and wise, cradles the Saperavi and whispers the secrets of time as the wine begins its transformation. Here, within the earthen womb, the grape's essence melds with the gentle embrace of the vessel, creating a wine that is both timeless and new.

Days turn into nights, and Ana tends to her creation with devotion. The Saperavi evolves, developing a bold persona—its flavors an embodiment of the region's soul. Picture a dance of dark berries and spices, a symphony of black cherries and plums, harmonizing with whispers of smoke and leather.

In every glass of our Saperavi wine, one discovers not just a libation but a glimpse into Ana's heart and the essence of Georgian winemaking. It's a celebration of heritage, a tribute to the land that nurtures us, and Ana's opus—a true testament to her tireless dedication and unyielding passion.

Join us in raising a glass to Ana Berikashvili's Saperavi, a wine that tells a story, a wine that is a reflection of ancient wisdom, and a wine that is an invitation to a journey through the vineyards of Georgia. Cheers to a wine that is Saperavi at its purest form.

With a gentle touch and a discerning eye, she selects the finest clusters, each a testament to the region's unique terroir and centuries of viticultural wisdom.

Once plucked, the Mtsvane grapes embark on a transformation, a metamorphosis guided by Ana's artistry and the whispers of the vineyards. In the heart of Anaberika Winery, they find solace in the qvevri—a sacred clay vessel where tradition and innovation intertwine.

Within the qvevri's comforting embrace, the Mtsvane grapes undergo a timeless dance. The vessel, an extension of the land itself, cradles the grapes as they evolve. Here, the essence of Mtsvane is released, a dance of vibrant aromas and delicate flavors.

As days turn into weeks and seasons change, Ana tends to her precious creation. The result is a wine that sings of Georgia—the freshness of citrus, the crispness of green apple, and the subtle embrace of herbs. Mtsvane becomes a symphony, each note telling a tale of the vine, the sun, and the soil. In every glass of our Mtsvane wine, one finds a piece of history, a tribute to Georgia's vinicultural legacy. It's a wine that embodies the soul of the land, a wine that Ana Berikashvili lovingly crafts—a masterpiece that is Mtsvane in its truest form.

Let us toast to Mtsvane, to Ana's devotion, and to the spirit of Georgian winemaking. Here's to a wine that is a journey through time, a wine that is Mtsvane at its purest, a testament to the artistry of the vine.
