Anaberika's Winery Experience: Wine Tasting, Gastronomic Delights, and Culinary Masterclasses

Discover the Essence of Georgia: An Exquisite Journey Through Anaberika Winery and Beyond

Embark on an immersive journey into the heart of Anaberika Winery, a sanctuary where tradition and artistry intertwine to craft exceptional wines. Our program invites you to not only witness but actively engage in the winemaking process. The day begins with a warm welcome to our vineyard, where the very soul of our wines originates. You'll stroll through sun-kissed vines, learning about the grape varieties that make our wines extraordinary. From the revered Rkatsiteli to the robust Saperavi, each grape tells a unique story. As the sun reaches its zenith, we gather for a leisurely lunch amid the picturesque vineyards. Here, the essence of Georgian hospitality unfolds as we share stories over a table laden with delectable, farm-to-table dishes. Imagine the flavors of Mtsvadi, the tantalizing aroma of Khachapuri, and the burst of freshness in a salad made from locally sourced ingredients. Buoyed by this sumptuous feast, we venture into the heart of winemaking - the qvevris. These ancient clay vessels cradle the grape's transformation into liquid gold. With Ana Berikashvili's expert guidance, you'll immerse yourself in the alchemy, from the grape's gentle pressing to the fermenting juice that promises the nectar of the gods. The day culminates in a tasting session, where you savor the fruits of your labor - our handcrafted wines. Each sip unravels a story of dedication, a dance of flavors on your palate. The Rkatsiteli, amber and soulful; the Saperavi, bold and robust; and the Mtsvane, a delicate embrace of the land.

As the sun reaches its zenith, we gather amidst the vibrant vineyards for a soul-enriching lunch. Picture succulent Mtsvadi grilling on an open flame, each sizzle echoing centuries of tradition. The aroma of this cherished barbecue, infused with local herbs and spices, is a prelude to the feast that awaits. At our table, discover the magic of Khachapuri - a warm, cheesy embrace that captures the essence of Georgia. This bread, shaped with care and brimming with flavor, is a testament to the craft that defines our cuisine. Paired with farm-fresh salads, a burst of colors and textures, it's a celebration of locally sourced goodness. And then, the pièce de résistance - Khinkali, the crown jewel of Georgian dumplings. Delicately pleated dough, enveloping a flavorful filling, each bite is a journey through the verdant hills of Kakheti. The juices that escape with that first succulent bite are a precursor to the symphony of flavors within.

As the meal draws to a close, we invite you to taste the sweetness of Georgia - Churchkhela. These handcrafted delights, resembling hanging candles, are nature's candy. Rich grape must and walnuts merge, creating a tantalizing treat, a perfect finale to this culinary escapade. If this culinary voyage through the flavors of Georgia intrigues your senses, we extend a heartfelt invitation to experience the magic in person. Join us and let your taste buds waltz through a culinary landscape as old as the vines themselves. Anaberika Winery awaits, where every meal is a sonnet, every wine a verse."
